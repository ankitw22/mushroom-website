EVERYTHING YOU NEED TO KNOW.

What is MCP? +
MCP (Model Context Protocol) is an open standard that lets AI models connect with external tools and data — a universal bridge for AI integrations. It enables AI assistants to take real actions in your apps.
What is Mushrooms MCP? +
Mushrooms MCP connects your AI to 2,000+ apps including Google Sheets through MCP, handling authentication, API limits, and security so you don't have to.
Can I use Mushrooms MCP with any AI platform? +
Yes. Mushrooms MCP works with any MCP-compatible client — Claude, ChatGPT, Cursor, Windsurf, GitHub Copilot, and more. One endpoint, universal compatibility.
How long does it take to set up integrations with Mushrooms MCP? +
Under 2 minutes. Get your MCP endpoint, choose your Google Sheets actions, paste the URL into your AI client, and you're ready to go.
Is MCP secure? +
Yes. Every MCP endpoint is secured with built-in authentication and encryption. Your data stays within your control boundary — nothing is stored or shared without your explicit permission.
Which applications can I integrate with Mushrooms MCP? +
2,000+ apps across CRM, marketing, project management, databases, communication tools, developer tools, and more — including Slack, Notion, Gmail, GitHub, HubSpot, and Google Workspace.
